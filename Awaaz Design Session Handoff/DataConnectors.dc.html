How your channels handle citizen data
Saarthi never surfaces citizen usernames on your dashboard's priority ranking. Identifiable information lives only inside the evidence drawer and only appears when you explicitly click through. Political sentiment classifiers are off by default. All processing runs on MEITY-empanelled infrastructure with data residency in India.
